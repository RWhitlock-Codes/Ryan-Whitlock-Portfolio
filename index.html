About me

Hello, my name is Ryan, and I am currently developing my skills to become a web developer. Even before a career transition, I found myself drawn to the process of coding websites in my spare time. The results and the sense of accomplishment gained from bringing my ideas to life, even in small ways, were rewarding. Following a period of re-evaluation, I have now made the decision to pursue web development as my primary career path. My immediate efforts are concentrated on building a strong foundation in front-end technologies, specifically HTML and CSS. I am also eager to expand my knowledge into version control using Git/GitHub, as well as learning JavaScript and modern frameworks such as React. For me, this journey is driven by the satisfaction of creating functional and visually engaging websites, and I am committed to continuous learning and growth in this field.
